Legend

Year

%Y
Year with century
%C
Year / 100
%y
Year % 100

Month

%m
Month number, zero-padded
%_m
Month number, blank-padded
%-m
Month number, no-padded
%B
Full month name
%b, %h
Abbreviated month name

Day

%A
Day of the week name
%a
Abbreviated day of the week name
%u
Day of the week (Monday is 1, 1..7)
%w
Day of the week (Sunday is 0, 0..6)
%d
Day of the Month, zero-padded
%-d
Day of the Month, no-padded
%e
Day of the Month, blank-padded
%j
Day of the Year, zero-padded

Time

%H
Hour of the day, 24-hour clock, zero-padded
%k
Hour of the day, 24-hour clock, blank-padded
%I
Hour of the day, 12-hour clock, zero-padded
%l
Hour of the day, 12-hour clock, blank-padded
%P
AM or PM
%p
am or pm
%M
Minute of the hour, zero-padded
%S
Second of the minute, zero-padded
%L, %3N
Millisecond of the second, zero-padded
%6N
Microsecond of the second
%N, %9N
Nanosecond of the second

Weeks

%G
The week-based year
%g
The last 2 digits of the week-based year
%V
Week number of the week-based year (01..53)
%U
Week number of the year. The week starts with Sunday.
%W
Week number of the year. The week starts with Monday.

Other

%z
Time zone as hour and minute offset from UTC
%Z
Time zone abbreviation name
%s
Number of seconds since Unix Epoch
%Q
Number of microseconds since Unix Epoch
%n
Newline
%t
Tab
%%
Percent

Combinations

%c
date and time (%a %b %e %T %Y)
%D, %x
Date (%m/%d/%y)
%F
The ISO 8601 date format (%Y-%m-%d)
%v
VMS date (%e-%b-%Y)
%r
12-hour time (%I:%M:%S %p)
%R
24-hour time (%H:%M)
%T, %X
24-hour time (%H:%M:%S)
%+
date(1) (%a %b %e %H:%M:%S %Z %Y)
